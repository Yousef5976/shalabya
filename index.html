Our Story

The Shalabya Story

Shalabya was born from a simple conviction: that Middle Eastern cuisine — rich, ancient, and endlessly varied — deserved a home where it could be celebrated without compromise. Founded in 2019 by the Shalabya family, the restaurant opened its doors in Cairo's cultural quarter with a menu that draws from the breadth of Arab culinary tradition.

Our executive chef, who trained across Egypt, Lebanon, and Morocco, has spent two decades refining a vocabulary that bridges memory and innovation. Every dish on our menu carries the weight of tradition while remaining alive to possibility — an heirloom technique given new breath, a familiar flavor placed in unexpected company.

We believe dining is one of the last great communal rituals. At Shalabya, we guard it carefully. The room is designed to slow time. The service is warm but never intrusive. The menu changes with the seasons so there is always something to discover.
